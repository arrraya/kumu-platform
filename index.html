Who I Am

The Thread

I started as a journalist and public affairs analyst — trained to find the story inside complex, noisy information. That instinct never left. What changed is the toolbox.

Over the years I moved from writing scripts and campaign narratives to building data pipelines, predictive models, and full-stack web applications. The common thread is always the same: take something complicated and make it legible for the people who need it.

I participated in political campaigns for the Constitutional Convention and the 2021 municipal elections, using AI tools, communication algorithms, and data analytics to shape strategy. Those campaigns were my first real laboratory for data-driven storytelling at scale.

Today, I build AI-powered communication artifacts — tools that sit at the intersection of data science, narrative design, and software engineering. Kumu Platform is the most complete expression of that: a football analytics system that turns raw match data into professional intelligence.
